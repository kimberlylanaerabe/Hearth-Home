My Hearth Home

A calm, personal workout tracker designed to feel like returning to a warm hearth — not chasing perfection.
My Hearth Home helps you build a sustainable relationship with movement. Instead of focusing on streaks, pressure, or performance metrics alone, this app is about consistency, reflection, and honoring different seasons of energy.

Some days you train hard.
Some days you rest.
Both belong here.

Why This Exists

Many fitness apps push intensity, streaks, and optimization. That works for some people — but not everyone.
My Hearth Home is built around a different philosophy:
Progress can be slow and still meaningful
Rest is part of growth
Tracking should feel grounding, not stressful

Your fitness journey can evolve over time
Think of it as a personal training journal that lives on your device.

Core Features

Current and planned features include:
Log workouts
Track exercises, sets, reps, and weight
View your past training sessions
Simple progress tracking
Personal notes and reflections
Flexible routines (not rigid programs)

Future ideas:
Multi-user support
Workout templates
Gentle reminders
Seasonal or mood-based training logs
Progress insights without pressure
Optional community spaces

Philosophy
This app is built around three ideas:

1. Consistency over intensity
Showing up matters more than pushing to extremes.

2. Cycles are normal
Energy, motivation, and strength naturally rise and fall.

3. Your journey is personal
No comparison. No leaderboard. Just your path.

Who This Is For

My Hearth Home may be a good fit if you:
Want a simple workout tracker
Feel overwhelmed by traditional fitness apps
Like journaling alongside training
Are returning to fitness after a break
Prefer a slower, sustainable approach
